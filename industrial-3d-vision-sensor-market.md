<p><p>The Industrial 3D Vision Sensor market is categorized into different types, including Photogrammetry Technology, Raster Structured Light, Digital Image Correlation, and others. Photogrammetry Technology uses cameras to capture multiple images and measure the positions of objects in 3D space. Raster Structured Light involves a projector projecting a pattern onto an object and a camera capturing the deformed pattern for 3D reconstruction. Digital Image Correlation relies on image comparison to measure object deformation. The "others" category includes various 3D vision sensor technologies that don't fit into the mentioned types.</p></p>
