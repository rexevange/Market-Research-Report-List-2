<p><p>The Vacuum Gauges for Semiconductor Equipment market offers various types of gauges to accurately measure and control vacuum levels in semiconductor manufacturing processes. Capacitance Diaphragm Gauge utilizes a diaphragm to measure pressure changes with high accuracy. Ionization Vacuum Gauge measures gas ionization in a vacuum to determine pressure levels. Pirani Vacuum Gauge uses the principle of heat transfer to measure pressure. Other types of gauges available include Hot Cathode Ionization Gauge, Cold Cathode Ionization Gauge, and Thermocouple Gauge, which offer different measurement mechanisms to suit specific requirements in semiconductor equipment.</p></p>

<p><p>Vacuum gauges are widely used in various applications within the semiconductor equipment market. During etching and cleaning, vacuum gauges assist in maintaining optimal pressure conditions, ensuring accurate removal of unwanted materials. In ion implantation, these gauges measure the required vacuum levels needed for ion beam formation. Additionally, vacuum gauges find applications in other processes such as metrology, lithography, and inspection, helping maintain the desired vacuum conditions for reliable semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
